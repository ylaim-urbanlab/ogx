Engine Rebuild Log — 2009 Subaru EJ257 (2.5L Boxer Turbo)
Date started: 2024-08-12
Date completed: 2024-10-03

Work performed:
- Bored cylinders to 0.020 oversize
- New forged pistons installed
- Head gaskets (MLS) replaced on both banks
- Valve stem seals replaced
- Oil pump replaced
- New rod bearings and main bearings
- Throttle body cleaned
- Intake and exhaust manifolds cleaned and re-torqued

Issues found:
- Cylinder 4 showed scoring, required more aggressive bore
- Original head bolts re-used (torque verified in spec)

Next service: Check for leaks at 1,000 miles, re-torque head bolts (if TTY, skip)
